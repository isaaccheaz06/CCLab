Specter

Scientific Name:

Jihe (几何)

Discovered by / at:

Mr. Isaac Cheaz on January 27, 2025

Family:

Specters are geometric entities that replicate the forms of creatures from different worlds. The Specter described here takes the shape of a butterfly. Although they mimic the forms of living beings, all Specters share a distinct feature: an exposed, beating heart. They inhabit a tranquil, dark dimension and react strongly when disturbed, creating illusions and presenting the illusion of multiple fused creatures.

Habitat:

The Specter resides in a dark dimension populated by geometric shapes. This environment is peaceful, with minimal illumination and occasional wormholes leading to other worlds. Specters sustain themselves on light—however minimal. There is no food chain, no predators or prey in this dimension; instead, Specters spend their entire lives wandering through the void, sometimes interacting with one another.

Appearance:

Specters take on the forms of beings from other dimensions. However, the only part of their body that is truly their own is their heart. The Specter documented here has copied the form of a butterfly. They are shy creatures—when approached, their heart glows bright red, and their heartbeat accelerates. While some Specters may have more complex designs with additional lines, the one discovered consists of nine circles and fourteen connecting lines. Despite mimicking other creatures, Specters lack the ability to replicate their colors and finer details, resulting in a hollow, skeletal appearance.

Key Characteristics:

  • A heart that beats faster when approached.
  • Highly geometric structure—transforms mimicked creatures into simplified but recognizable geometric forms.
  • Creates illusions when disturbed.
  • Wanders through the abyss, occasionally interacting with other Specters.
  • Requires light to survive, though only in small amounts.

What we know so far about Specters? Specters are solitary beings that roam the abyss, occasionally encountering others of their kind. They are naturally formless, only taking shape when passing through wormholes into other dimensions. Their forms are simplified, "geometricized" versions of the creatures they mimic, retaining the same movement and behaviors as their originals. The most distinct feature of all Specters is their exposed, beating heart—the one trait they all share.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Move your mouse close to its heart to make it beat faster and turn red, signifying the creature’s nervousness. You can also interact with the environment—dragging your mouse (while holding down click) will create ripples in the background, disturbing the Specter and prompting it to react by generating illusions.
